Our courses

Welcome to the best place to learn about HR & L&D

1. CIPD Level 3 Foundation in People Practice

Nothing builds confidence and credibility like a qualification. Whether you’d like a refresher, you’re looking to expand your knowledge, or you’re starting a career in the people profession, the Level 3 Foundation Certificate in People Practice will help you progress. You’ll gain the capabilities you need to attract new talent and maintain your organisation’s ability to do right by your people, or kick start your brand-new career.

2. CIPD Level 5 Associate Diploma in People Practice

Get the tools you need to take the next step in your career, as well as a confidence and skills boost. For those with experience, this qualification is internationally recognised and will support career progression. Building on your existing knowledge, you’ll get a sound understanding of how HR fits into achieving strategic objectives and how data can be used to improve performance. This Level 5 Diploma, as with all of CIPD’s updated qualifications, is based on the CIPD Profession Map. It defines the knowledge, behaviours and inherent values underpinning today’s people profession.

3. CIPD Level 5 Associate Diploma in Organisational Learning & Development

Inspire learning and development within your organisation by completing a qualification in L&D. The CIPD Level 5 Diploma in Organisational Learning and Development is the most comprehensive course available for L&D professionals, ideal for you if you want to formalise your existing experience, skills and knowledge. You’ll study the core knowledge and behaviours any People Professional needs and extend these through specific units to further specialise in your field.

4. HR and Learning & Development Apprenticeships

Let your team unleash its potential with an HR or L&D Apprenticeship. Employ somebody new or upskill your workforce. With CIPD qualifications and an expert learning community, your HR apprentice will quickly become an important member of your team. Whatever your needs, an HR apprenticeship programme is ideal for everyone from entry-level to those with their eyes on more responsibility.

How we deliver our courses

Our content is built and delivered by subject-matter experts and learning designers. It’s always relevant, bite-sized and engaging.

Throughout your learning journey at Avado you will experience our unique learning methodology which has been built to give you the most effective and valuable experience possible. During your journey you will interact with various experienced and knowledgeable tutors from our faculty team who will guide, support and enhance your knowledge and understanding around the various topics and elements of your course. The tutoring team is made of subject matter experts assigned to relevant units to allow you to gain the very latest insights and information around the specific units of study.
